Welcome to the Seventh Wonder of Atenveldt! We are the Barony of Granite Mountain, a local branch of the Society for Creative Anachronism (S.C.A.) covering Prescott, Prescott Valley, Chino Valley and the surrounding areas. If you are outside this area you may want to visit our sister Baronies. To the north of us you’ll find the Shire of Windale and the Barony of Ered Sul, along with the College of Sankt Vladmir. To the south of us, in the Phoenix Metro area you’ll find the Barony of Atenveldt, the Barony of Sundragon and the Barony of Twin Moons, along with the College of Brymstonne. The Barony is just one group within the Kingdom of Atenveldt.

​The SCA is an international organization dedicated to researching and re-creating the arts and skills of pre-17th-century Europe. Our "Known World" consists of 19 kingdoms, with over 30,000 members residing in countries around the world. Members, dressed in clothing of the Middle Ages and Renaissance, attend events which feature tournaments, royal courts, feasts, dancing, various classes & workshops, and more.

Look around the site, if you are new to the SCA, check out the Newcomer's FAQ. If you need more direct feedback, check out the officer list here or email the Seneschal. Take a look at the BGM Facebook page or join our (unofficial) Baronial Email list.
